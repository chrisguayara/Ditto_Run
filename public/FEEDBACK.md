What is the game missing ?

Too deterministic:  lack of skill expression and player decision in a puzzle platformer

Players are given no reason to transform, you are just expected to transform to progress anyways so theres no problem solving nor any choices as you cant surpass the level if you dont transform. Game is far too scripted. 

Players must be able to pick and choose which transformation to use to get there fastest but all are availlable, some are better than other. Not all have to be available, but there should be someway to choose a previous form in each map.  

No defined enough:

is this a puzzle game ? a platformer? resource manager with the energy?

What i learned from hyun and jj's game: fun to move around, unpredictable, has skill expression, clear defined goal and a need to try again to want to do better than previous round. Game is defined by a need to get to the end , controls are limited and defined, physics does most of the work. 

Our game seems to be fun from just the exploration and art like the content itself is cool , but its not defined enough.

How we can change this:
- Lets strip the fat and any boring elements
- Energy feels restrictive instead of a fun resource management. It adds to the deterministic feeling of the game. 
- Ditto isnt necessary hes just a vessel, even if we were to keep designing the game around him as we move forward he will always just be inferior to any transformation. 
- Rowlet and Phantump are boring, phantump is cool in terms of being different from rowlet for a few seconds then he gets boring again when all else he can do is vertical movement and phasing walls
- Lets follow the rule of cool: Greninja is dope and by far our most fun pokemon. Lets add one more pokemon and thats it . I think charizard works well as he is a starter so recognizeable but mostly because I have a moveset in mind for him and hes cool. 
- Too many pokemon clutters map design, and makes it hard to design for multiple pokemon without any coherence between each sections as only one character is needed

Greninja is our way forward as we can very much design the map around him, and he is fun already even with the jank in his moveset. He is a great way to pivot. I want to make him the central character.
